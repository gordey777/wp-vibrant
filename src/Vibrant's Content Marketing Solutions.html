    Content Marketing Solutions

    Vibrant’s content marketing solutions help marketers amplify their paid, owned and earned content in a native environment at scale. It allows brands to showcase their great content in the right context for maximum consumer engagement.

    Content Distribution

    Designed for consumers to either click and consume content on a brand’s owned platform or social channels, to raise awareness.
    • Brands have a dedicated unit to promote their content to ensure maximum brand exposure.
    • Traffic is directed back to the content hub or site and is charged on a CPC basis.

    Content Curation & Creation

    Designed for consumers to engage with curated brand and editorial content without leaving the publisher page.
    • Vibrant’s content community can create original and engaging content for all audiences.
    • Content is curated and hosted in full screen, interactive formats
