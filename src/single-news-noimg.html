    Vibrant’s brand safety guarantee!

    The stats speak for themselves

    We’ve all seen the recent headlines relating to brand safety. A headline which started with the Times claiming brands were ‘funding terrorism’ suddenly became a huge concern for the industry.

    A subject which a mere two months ago may not have even crossed brands and agencies minds were catapulted to a high-profile and top priority issue, which led to many top advertisers including Vodafone, Sky and the UK Government pulling their ad spend.

    Whilst in the last couple of weeks we have seen big social platforms apologise and put several measures in place to reassure brands that the issue is being dealt with, it is still a problem that is now on pretty much all brands and agency planners agendas, and is something that we need to address as an industry. The recent news, the ambiguous nature of programmatic placement, plus issues such as bot traffic continuing to rise means it’s not surprising that brands are starting to feel the real threat, and it is down to digital media owners and ad tech players to fill them with confidence when it comes to brand safety.

     

    BRAND SAFETY GUIDELINES

    Since 2000, Vibrant Media has been proactively tackling the challenges to ensure brand safe and trusted placements for all our advertisers. We take pride in placing brand safety at the core of what we do, which is why brands continue to trust and work with us over 17 years later. With a sound brand safety guideline firmly established, Vibrant ensures all brand advertising is natively placed within premium, relevant editorial and that all campaigns are monitored from the initial set up right through to completion. Working with both man and machine, we proactively check campaigns daily allowing brands to feel safe in the knowledge that their advertising will only ever be seen by a person in the right place, at the right time.

     

    THIRD PARTY VERIFICATION

    Since 2013, Vibrant has been working with MOAT to analyse ad impressions and content, and to measure viewability and attention – but it doesn’t stop there. We also partner with MOAT to provide third-party verification that our impressions are safe.

    Our RTC technology and safe serve system work right down to a granular level to ensure we protect brands and only target the right words and images in the right context. Being DTSG and TAG certified also helps us to maintain a safe environment for brands as we continue to move into the future of digital.

    For more information on Vibrant’s premium brand safety policy, please reach out to your Vibrant rep who will be more than happy to share content and details with you.
