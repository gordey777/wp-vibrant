      Life at Vibrant Profile

    What do you do?

    Client Services Specialist in the San Francisco office.

    Describe your team in three words?

    Team Client Services: Best-in-class
    Team SF: We like cats

    What attracted you to Vibrant and what keeps you here?

    I first joined Vibrant as an intern during the summer months before my last year of college. My experience from being an intern is definitely what motivated me to come back to – and to stay with – Vibrant. I thought it would be a stereotypical internship – coffee runs, monotonous daily tasks, painfully long hours, etc. – but after being put in challenging, thought-provoking situations and given business-critical assignments on the first day, I realized how great the summer would be. After working through the summer on exciting projects, with a tight-knit and extremely outgoing team, I knew Vibrant would be a great place for full-time employment! Upon graduation, I was offered a job as a client services specialist and all of the great qualities from my internship have now become part of my daily life.

    What makes someone Vibrant?

    One trait that all Vibrant employees share is a willingness to be outgoing. We all come from different walks of life and different parts of the globe – becoming part of the Vibrant family involves engraining yourself within a diverse group of exuberant people that constantly try new things and always have a great time. Try not to get left behind 😉

    What daily tasks do you enjoy most and why?

    Collaborating with the sales team and campaign management team on campaign strategy because, as our famous director of campaign management says, “teamwork to make the dream work.”

    What do you like to do outside of work?

    Sporting events, day trips to Santa Cruz, and party with friends

    What’s your party trick?

    You’ll have to party with me to find out!

    What do you do?

    I’m the Director of Account Management in the LA region, which means that I work alongside Sales to manage all Strategic Accounts.  Also, I serve as the sales lead of our the Texas territory.

    Describe your team in three words?

    Connected, Fun, and Collaborative

    What attracted you to Vibrant and what keeps you here?

    I’ve been at Vibrant for almost 5 years and what initially attracted me to Vibrant was the fact that I’d get to work in a fast-paced environment where I’d get to evangelize a cool, unique digital ad offering. Vibrant’s continued innovation keeps me engaged, but my awesome team is one of the main reasons why I’m happy here.

    What daily tasks do you enjoy most and why?

    Given the generally flat organization here, many employees here (including myself) get to wear a number of hats towards our collective goals.  For me, that includes prospecting new business, negotiating terms, closing deals, managing campaign setup, pulling actionable insights, mentoring/training other AMs.  I truly enjoy working alongside other AMs and Sellers, because I continuously learn new things from them. This interaction allows me to impart what I know to others.

    Tell us something no one knows about you?

    I think that the 80s produced the greatest decade of music, across all genres (Rock/R&B/etc.) Phil Collins in the MAN – HAHAHA.

    What do you do?

    Director, Publisher Solutions managing the Tech, Automotive, Gaming, Sports and Men’s verticals

    Describe your team in three words?

    Dynamic, adaptable, solution-orientated

    What attracted you to Vibrant and what keeps you here?

    I was attracted by the chance to be involved in something different from the rest of the industry, and with ad solutions where I saw the value for consumers. After 6 years that remains true, but also the people and the atmosphere have kept me engaged and enthused.

    What daily tasks do you do, and what do you enjoy the most?

    Establish and maintain relationships with publishers in order to increase reach for the sales team. I enjoy this the most when I am able to create mutually beneficial business partnerships with publishers.

    Tell us something no one else knows about you?

    I’m pretty handy in the kitchen

    What do you do?

    I’m an Account Executive on the NY Sales team. I actively cover several agencies in the NY, Philadelphia, and Atlanta regions, manage my own book of business of existing customers and seek opportunities to bring in new business.

    Describe your team in three words?

    We Crush Media

    What attracted you to Vibrant and what keeps you here?

    What initially attracted me to Vibrant was the rare opportunity to join a company that has established itself as a leader in the industry for 16 years, and the quality of mentorships I would receive from an accomplished management team.

    The thing that keeps me here, which most people agree on, is the culture. Vibrant has a unique ability to provide you with professional growth while having the time of your life with people you genuinely enjoy spending time with.

    What daily tasks do you enjoy most and why?

    My daily tasks include managing client relationships, prospecting new clients, pitching Vibrant and hosting client events.

    Of these tasks, my favorite thing to do is pitch Vibrant during client meetings and receive market feedback in person.

    Tell us something no one else knows about you?

    There was a period in my life where I refused to leave my house without my Batman cape on. It lasted longer that I’d like to admit…

     

    What do you do?

    Account Director – Detroit

    Describe your team in three words?

    Efficient. Creative. Funny.

    What attracted you to Vibrant and what keeps you here?

    Our native, contextual ad solutions PLUS working in a team environment.

    What makes someone Vibrant?

    Lively. A person that can light up a room, and bring joy to all they are around.

    What daily tasks do you enjoy most and why?

    Collaborating with our team daily gets me pumped up. I love pinging new ideas off of people on our team, hearing their ideas and experiences, and the energy around bringing new ideas to life for our clients.

    What do you like to do outside of work?

    Spending most of my time with my family – my wife, two daughters and our German Shephard. Playing guitar when I can find the time!

    What’s your party trick?

    Sadly, it’s the Irish Fade. (Defined: “when you leave a party/bar/etc. without telling your friends, typically because you’re afraid they won’t let you leave. it’s kind of a “better to ask for forgiveness than for permission” kind of thing.”) I’m really good at it.

    What do you do?

    I’m a graphic designer within the marketing team. I collaborate with my team to plan and produce sales/marketing material and custom collateral for clients and events. I’m also responsible for ensuring a consistent brand identity across all business communications.

    Describe your team in three words?

    Creative, Passionate, Adaptable

    What attracted you to Vibrant and what keeps you here?

    I was attracted to Vibrant by the unique culture and opportunity to work alongside an awesome team, and to be creative without limits – all of which are what have kept me here.

    What daily tasks do you enjoy most and why?

    My daily tasks include designing and keeping up-to-date sales/marketing material and designing/ordering/distributing marketing collateral.

    Tell us something no one else knows about you.

    I’ve been taking design classes and Parsons over the past year to earn a certificate in Graphic and Digital Design, which I’ve really enjoyed. The experience has helped me better my technical skills and gives me a different perspective on design outside of what I do at work.
